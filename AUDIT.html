The 60-second summary

If you only read one paragraph: most of the trading core works — analysis, signals, charts, position sizing, MT5 EA — they all use real APIs and real prices. The problems are in the framing around them: a few pages still show fake numbers, the SELL signal calculator displays "+-X%", four backend endpoints don't require a login, and the alerts page lies about the unread count. The good news: nothing dangerous is hiding in the trading math itself. The bad news: a beginner reading the Market, News, or Alerts pages will think they're seeing live information when they're not.
