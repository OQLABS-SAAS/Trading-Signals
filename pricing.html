Common questions
Can I cancel anytime? ↓
Yes. Cancel anytime from your settings page. No lock-in, no cancellation fees. Your account downgrades to Free at the end of the billing period.
What does "exchange connection" mean? ↓
You connect your exchange API key (read-only) to DotVerse. This lets DotVerse sync your real portfolio positions automatically — no more manual entry. DotVerse never has permission to place trades on your behalf.
Is my API key secure? ↓
API keys are encrypted at rest. We only request read permissions — DotVerse cannot place, modify, or cancel orders on your exchange. You can revoke access at any time from your settings page.
What's the difference between Pro and Elite for signals? ↓
The signal engine is identical. Elite adds the risk management layer on top — position tracking, VaR, stress testing, and cross-asset correlation. If you only need signals and scanner, Pro is the right tier.
Do you offer a free trial of Pro? ↓
The Free tier lets you evaluate signal quality before committing. We don't offer time-limited Pro trials, but our Free tier has no expiry — take as long as you need.
